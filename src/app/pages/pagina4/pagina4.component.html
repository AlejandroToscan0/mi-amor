HOW I MEET YOU?

I know I remember many things, but to be honest, I don't remember the day or the exact moment when we met, I only remember falling in love with you at first sight, I was a kid, I didn't know what it was like to like someone much less fall in love at first sight, but with you I will always be grateful to life and destiny for having put you in my way. ..

Eventually, I lost track of you, but I knew you were still there, I would see you on the street, I wanted to say hello but you were not alone.., that's when I realized that life was telling me that I had already lost..
